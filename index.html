Georgiy Zantaraia

Ukrainian judoka

Georgiy Malkhazovich Zantaraya (born October 21, 1987, Gali) is a Ukrainian judoka of Georgian origin, participant in the Summer Olympic Games, winner and medalist of the World and European Championships, Honored Master of Sports.

Married, has a son, David (born July 5, 2012), daughter, Eva (born 2018).

The first coach was V.V. Briman, then - V.V. Dubrova (Ukrainian)

He plays for the sports society "Dynamo" (Kyiv). The only Ukrainian judoka to win the title of world champion in judo (in the entire history of both independent Ukraine and the Union Republic of the USSR).

The National Olympic Committee of Ukraine twice (in August 2009 and May 2011) recognized Georgiy Zantarai as the best athlete in the country.

Major of the Security Service of Ukraine (SBU). Deputy of the Kyiv City Council (elected from the Servant of the People party).
